Truman Ports // Eugene Lang The New School for Liberal Arts // Senior studying Journalism + Design.

Editor-in-Chief of The New School Free Press, the student-run newspaper at The New School.

Truman currently lives in Bed-Stuy, Brooklyn after moving from Oakland, California three years ago for college.

Although his primary interest lies in writing arts and culture pieces, he has experience covering breaking news, protests, rallies, and formulating well-researched political opinion pieces.

Truman has contributed both to Interview Magazine and V Magazine's online content.
